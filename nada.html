Nada Grocery

Nada Grocery is a grocery store dedicated to reducing waste. Their mission is to inspire people to change the way they shop for groceries by reducing all kinds of packaging such as plastic. Nada asks you to bring your own containers that will be weighed in store and given a dishwasher-tag that can stay with your container. They also have their own cafe inside the store where you can have lunch after shopping.

They have their own website, facebook page, and instagram where you can find more information about who they are and the impact such a store has on the world.
