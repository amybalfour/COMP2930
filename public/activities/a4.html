Waste Reduction Week 2019

October 21-27, 2019

Waste Reduction Week, held by the Recycling Council of British Columbia, is happening for the 18th year in October. During this week, try as hard as you can to live a Zero Waste lifestyle. If you want to host any waste reduction events during this time, make sure to visit the page.
