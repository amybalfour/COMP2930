Pop-Up Junk Drop

Sunday, May 26, 2019 - 10:00am - 4:00pm

Surrey Operations Centre

Bring valid ID

If you are a Surrey resident, you can bring your junk (up to one-ton) to be recycled and reused. Items such as furniture, electronics, small appliances, scrap metal, and lighting products are being accepted. Salvation Army and Diabetes Canada are gathering these items.
