White Spot

White Spot is a well know restaurant chain in BC. What is well known however, is how White Spot sources most of their ingredients locally and drinks have a straw-by-request-only policy. The amount of waste a popular restaurant reduces through these policies is huge and is a big step towards environment sustainability. They have 90 years of history and are strong givers of the community, giving support to many charities such as Zajac Ranch for Children.

They have their own website, facebook page, and instagram where you can learn about their menu or what else they do for our community.
