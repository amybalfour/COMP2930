Lupii Cafe

Lupii Cafe is a Zero Waste paradise. From the floors of the cafe to the containers used for food, everything in sight is made from reusable/reused materials.
The tables used for eating are actually made from the floor of an old gymnasium! Lupii isn't just a cafe however, it is a Zero Waste space dedicated to the world's sustainability.
They host community events, fundraise for charitable causes and interact with the people around them.

They have their own website, facebook page, and instagram where they post regularly about current community events and meals available.
