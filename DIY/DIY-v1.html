DIY & Lifestyle

In the modern world, it's no secret that humans like to frequently obtain new and exciting things. But where do these products go when they lose their fleeting appeal? In 2018, 717,906 tonnes of waste was accepted into the Vancouver Landfill. What if we could cut back on this waste by reusing or recycling its components, or, even better, never contribute to it in the first place? Here we have a gathering of recipes and instructions on how you can make your own products at home, thus avoiding all the waste of shipping, packaging, and more.
